    Privacy policy

    Last updated: 26.02.2026

    Heaffen (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website and services (the “Platform”).

    By using Heaffen, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.


    1. Information We Collect

    We may collect the following types of information:


    1.1 Information You Provide Directly

    When you:

    • Submit a candidate profile
    • Post a job listing
    • Fill out a contact form
    • Upload a CV
    • Contact us via email

    We may collect:

    • Full name
    • Email address
    • Phone number / Telegram / LinkedIn
    • Location
    • Professional information
    • Salary expectations
    • Work preferences
    • Resume files (PDF)
    • Company information (for employers)

    1.2 Automatically Collected Information

    When you use the platform, we may automatically collect:

    • IP address
    • Browser type
    • Device type
    • Operating system
    • Pages visited
    • Time spent on pages
    • Referring URLs

    This information helps us improve the platform and user experience.


    2. How We Use Your Information

    We use collected information to:

    • Publish candidate profiles and job listings
    • Connect candidates and employers
    • Improve platform functionality
    • Respond to inquiries
    • Send important service updates
    • Prevent fraud and abuse
    • Comply with legal obligations
    • We do not sell personal data to third parties.

    3. Public Information

    If you submit a candidate profile or job listing, certain information may be publicly displayed on the platform, including:

    • Professional title
    • Experience level
    • Skills
    • Salary expectations
    • Company details (for employers)

    Sensitive contact details may be hidden depending on platform settings.


    4. Data Sharing

    We may share your data only:

    • With employers (for candidate profiles)
    • With candidates (for job listings)
    • With service providers who help operate the platform (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
    • If required by law
    • All third-party providers are required to protect your data.

    5. Data Storage & Security

    We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against:

    • Unauthorized access
    • Alteration
    • Disclosure
    • Destruction

    However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.


    6. Data Retention

    We retain your personal data:

    • For as long as your profile or listing is active
    • As necessary to comply with legal obligations
    • Until you request deletion

    You may request deletion of your data at any time.


    8. Cookies

    We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

    • Improve user experience
    • Analyze traffic
    • Remember user preferences

    You can manage cookies through your browser settings.


    9. Third-Party Links

    The platform may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., LinkedIn, company websites). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.


    10. Children’s Privacy

    Heaffen is not intended for individuals under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.


    11. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

    Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
